# UMV2001.org - Project Domain & Business Context Document

## Project Overview

UMV2001.org is the official website and member management system of the **UMV 2001 O/L Alumni Association**.

The association consists of former students who studied in **Udadumbara Maha Vidyalaya** (currently known as **Udadumbara Secondary School**) and sat for the **G.C.E. Ordinary Level Examination in 2001**.

The association is not a commercial organization.

It is a community-based welfare society that works to maintain lifelong relationships among members and contribute positively to society, especially within the Udadumbara area.

The website serves two primary purposes:

1. Public-facing website for promoting the association and its social initiatives.
2. Private member management system for association members and administrators.

# Membership Management Philosophy

The membership module is one of the most important parts of the system.

The association wants a centralized record of all members.

Each member has:

* Membership number
* Personal information
* Contact information
* Family information
* Banking/payment information

The system should act as the organization's digital membership register.

---

# Family Information Purpose

Family information is collected for welfare and communication purposes.

Examples:

* Emergency contact situations
* Welfare assistance
* Maintaining accurate member records

Family information is strictly private.

Family information must never be publicly visible.

Only:

* The member
* Authorized administrators

may access this information.

---

# Payment Management Philosophy

The association collects a monthly membership fee from members.

The purpose is to fund:

* Welfare activities
* Social programs
* Community initiatives
* Event expenses

Payments are currently managed manually.

Administrators record payments after receiving them.

The system is not currently required to process online payments.

Instead:

* Admin records payments.
* Members view payment history.

Future online payment integration should be possible.

Examples:

* PayHere
* Bank transfer verification
* Payment gateway integration

The database design should support future expansion.

---

# Events Management Philosophy

Events are a core component of the association.

The website should preserve the history of all events.

Each event should contain:

* Title
* Description
* Location
* Event date
* Duration (optional)
* Photo gallery

The event system should support:

## Upcoming Events

Events planned for the future.

## Completed Events

Events already conducted.

## Historical Archive

Older events should remain accessible.

The website acts as a permanent record of the association's activities.

# Public Website Content Strategy

The public website should communicate:

* Professionalism
* Trustworthiness
* Community impact

The website should not feel corporate.

It should feel:

* Friendly
* Community-focused
* Educational
* Respectful

Tone:

* Warm
* Positive
* Community-oriented

Avoid:

* Aggressive marketing language
* Sales-oriented language
* Commercial terminology

Because this is a welfare society, not a business.

---

# Privacy Expectations

Privacy is extremely important.

The following information must never be public:

* Member addresses
* Phone numbers
* Email addresses
* Family information
* Bank account information
* Payment history

Only authorized users may access these records.

The website should follow privacy best practices even though the organization is relatively small.
